About Me

My name is Alistair Rowden I am originally from the United Kingdom and have had an interest in IT and computers since an early age, I was mainly into building computers and tinkering with software and games, my first foray into the programming/web development world was back in college when I was learning about HTML, back then I had little interest in the subject but it slowly grew on me in later years.

In my spare time I mostly hang out with my wife, our cat and her family, also from time to time I build ATV's & dirtbikes to blow off steam and race them around trails near where I live, I am also an avid mechanic working on countless cars and projects, I edit photos and design logos with Adobe Photoshop and most of all I have an extensive background making music under the alias "Katakai" on Soundcloud, I have been making music in FL Studio for over 6 years and have amassed a huge understanding of how to make and process each and every sound.
